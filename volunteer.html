Join With Us

If You Interest! You Can Join With Us As A Volunteer.

Volunteer Responsibilities

  • Support families and children in need
  • Assist in community events and workshops
  • Help with educational and peace-building programs
  • Spread awareness about our mission

Time Commitment

We welcome both short-term and long-term volunteers. Most roles require a minimum of 4 hours per week, but we're flexible to fit your schedule!

Who Can Join?

  • Passion for helping others
  • Good communication skills
  • Willingness to learn and collaborate
  • Age 18+ (or with parental consent)
  • Commitment to our values of respect and inclusion
